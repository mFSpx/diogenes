It grows by consent.  
It learns by evidence.  
It acts through permission.  
It speaks through conversation.  
It improves through use.  
It preserves truth internally so the Operator can experience ease externally.

Its metabolism is DBOS.  
Its memory is temporal, evidentiary, and conserved.  
Its tools are a sovereign proof hoard.  
Its generated surfaces are instruction compilers.  
Its graph is promoted, not hallucinated.  
Its claims carry provenance.  
Its actions require authority.  
Its interface is conversation.

LUCIDOTA exists to make the hard truth durable inside the machine so the human outside the machine can move with less friction, less loss, and more power.

The Operator commands.  
The system remembers.  
The symbiote grows.
