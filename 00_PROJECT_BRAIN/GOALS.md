# LUCIDOTA Goals

## Long-Term Goal

Get this system done: a working, verified, private, sovereign local LUCIDOTA/DIOGENES stack that can ingest, reason, retrieve, automate, and assist without becoming a pile of Markdown, vibes, or disconnected experiments.

## Operating Meaning

- Prefer working code over architectural noise.
- Keep planning and personal-assistant notes as support process until explicitly promoted into tested runtime behavior.
- Maintain kernel/interface/database boundaries.
- Keep the system private by default.
- Keep moving during background waits by switching to non-overlapping work.
- Commit and push verified progress frequently enough that state survives interruption.
