# Investigation Lifecycle

## Signal From User Paste

Use the lifecycle as architecture input. Correction: `Krampus Express` is canonical internal terminology, not optional metaphor.

## Loop

1. Ingest raw material.
2. Hash, timestamp, and record provenance immediately.
3. Normalize disparate inputs into comparable internal representations.
4. Extract entities, relationships, claims, evidence refs, and pivots.
5. Write graph/ontology records.
6. Generate inferences, hypotheses, and new pivots.
7. Re-enter ingest with the new pivots.
8. Repeat with audit trails intact.

## Why It Matters

- Chain of custody starts before interpretation.
- Normalization prevents apples-to-oranges comparison errors.
- Graph storage preserves relationship context, not just facts.
- Claims and inferences stay testable against evidence.
- Pivots make investigation recursive rather than linear.
