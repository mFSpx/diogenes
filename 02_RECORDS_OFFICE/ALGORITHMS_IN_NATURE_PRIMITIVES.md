# Algorithms in Nature Primitives

Local wrappers added under `/ALGOS`:

1. Distributed leader election: randomized maximal independent set for local graph neighborhoods.
2. Fold-change detection: ratio-sensitive feed-forward update equations.
3. Voronoi partitioning: nearest-seed region assignment.
4. Minimum-cost tree scoring: material length plus root path length penalty.
5. Physarum conductance: flux and conductance update.
6. Common-store feedback: resource-rate update and duration signal.
7. Sparse WTA tags: expansion plus k-winner mask and Hamming comparison.
8. Infotaxis: expected-entropy action selection.
9. Fisher localization: off-axis sensing score.
10. Decreasing-rate pruning: early high-prune schedule that freezes over time.

These are primitive math wrappers, not product policy. Product integration remains gated by the relevant workflow profile.
